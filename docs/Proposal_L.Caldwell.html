Emissions During the Covid-19 Pandemic

This map is important is relevant to what has become our new "normal". Covid-19 has been a relatively new illness ever since the first case in 2020 Wuhan, China. Scientists have still been trying to figure out this deadly disease and trying to find a cause. It can also tie in to our emission rates. Ever since majority of the world went into a nationwide or statewide lockdown, we were not able to go about our everyday lives anymore. Therefore, most of our emissions declined dramatically. Climate change has been an increasing topic of discussion years prior as our world continues to heat up. Could this deadly disease turnout to help global warming start to reverse?
